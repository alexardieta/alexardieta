Projects

STR Research

Description: Qualitative research and insight as to if long-term rental landlords would be less incentivized to turn over affordable housing for third-party short-term rental units through local policy/fiscal support. Data is collected through one-on-one interviews with legislative officials, renters, and long-term landlords to discuss potential legislative solutions to the unsustainable rise in short-term rentals

Manuscript on the Political Economy Assistantship

Description: I worked alongside faculty members to prepare a manuscript on the development of radical political economics. I contributed by proof reading, editting chapters, organizing authors' contributions, and formatting the manuscript in adherence to publisher guidelines.
